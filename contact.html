Do You Have Cards or Autographs of Intrest to Cardboard Conservation?

I am ALWAYS looking for new items for my collection, but also love to see items you may have even if they are not for sale. If you have any of the following items for sale or just to share with us please use the buttom below to email quality scans or photographs.

What I am interested in:

  • Cards or autographs of members of the Baseball Hall of Fame.
  • Cards from rare baseball sets.
  • Cards or autographs of athletes that match any of the following criteria.
    • Was born in Nebraska.
    • Graduated from a Nebraska High School.
    • Lived a considerable segment of their life in Nebraska.
    • Competed in intercollegiate sports at a college or university located in Nebraska.
    • Competed for any amatuer, junior, or developmental league while representing the state or a Nebraska city.
