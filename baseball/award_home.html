League MVP Winners

A Most Valuable Player award has been bestowed as far back as 1911, however baseball was inconsistent in awarding prior to 1931. Since that time both the National and American Leagues have named an MVP each season.

I am currently building a composite featuring a card of each league MVP issued in the year in which they won the award. In years were I have not yet acquired a card for the MVP a generic image of them will be shown in the gallery. Out of respect to my collecting budget, at this time I am only building this composite for league winners from 1950 to currrent.

Cy Young Award Winners

The Cy Young Award was first awarded at end of the 1956 season. From 1956-1966 it was awarded to the pitcher voted as having had the most outstanding individual season performance. In 1967, the award was expanded to honor one pitcher from each league.

I am currently building a composite featuring a card of each Cy Young Winner issued in the year in which they won the award. In years were I have not yet acquired a card for the winner a generic image of them will be shown in the gallery.

All-Star Game MVP Winners

Major Leauge Baseball hosted its first All-Star Game in 1933. However, no one was ever official named as an MVP of an All-Star Game until 1962. In that year, MLB hosted a pair of All-Star games and an MVP was named for both. After that season only one All-Star game has been hosted and an MVP has been name for every one of them except for 2002 when the game was allowed to end in a tie.

I am currently building a composite featuring a card of each All-Star Game MVP issued in the year in which they won the award. In years were I have not yet acquired a card for the winner a generic image of them will be shown in the gallery.
